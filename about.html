About Me

Senior Android Engineer building high-traffic apps for millions of users.

I'm Temir Atambayev, a Senior Android Engineer based in Auckland, New Zealand with over 8 years of experience building mobile applications that serve millions of users. My career spans market leaders like Trade Me - New Zealand's largest online marketplace - as well as HudApp, one of the world's fastest-growing dating platforms available in 145 countries.

I specialise in modernising legacy codebases, scaling architecture with MVVM and MVI patterns, and adopting modern UI toolkits like Jetpack Compose. Whether it's integrating payment systems, leading performance optimisations, or shipping features end-to-end, I care deeply about code quality and seamless user experience.

Beyond engineering, I enjoy mentoring junior developers and conducting code reviews. I've also delivered Android training courses and have a background in computer science from Nazarbayev University. If you have a project in mind or want to collaborate, I'd love to hear from you.
