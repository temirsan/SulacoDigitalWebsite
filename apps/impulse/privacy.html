Privacy Policy

Impulse Reader

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sulaco Digital ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Impulse Reader ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and any optional profile details.

Reading preferences: The App stores your saved sources, reading history, and preferences locally on your device. We may sync this data to our servers if you enable cloud sync.

Usage data: We may collect anonymised analytics about feature usage to improve the App. This data does not identify you personally.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the App, personalise your reading experience, deliver synced content across your devices, and respond to support requests.

4. Third-Party Content

Impulse Reader fetches articles and feeds from third-party websites. When you access this content, those websites may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We have no control over, and are not responsible for, third-party data practices.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored on secure servers using industry-standard encryption. Reading data is primarily stored on your device unless cloud sync is enabled.

6. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account data at any time through the App settings or by contacting us. You may also request a full export of your data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by email.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@sulacodigital.com.
