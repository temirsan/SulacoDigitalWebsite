Impulse
Reader

An innovative reader for news and articles. Minimal design, maximum focus. Read what matters, without the noise.

▶ App Store ▶ Google Play

Impulse Reader brings together your favourite sources — news sites, blogs, and RSS feeds — into one clean, distraction-free reading experience. No algorithms, no ads, no noise. Just the articles you want to read.

The reading mode strips away everything except the text and images that matter. Adjust the font size, switch between light and dark themes, and save articles to read offline whenever you want.

Impulse respects your time and your attention. Follow topics, not feeds. Available on iOS and Android.
