Privacy Policy

Spectral

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sulaco Digital ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Spectral ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and any profile details you provide.

Usage data: We may collect anonymised analytics data about how you use the App (e.g. which features are used most frequently) to improve the product. This data does not identify you personally.

Device information: We may collect basic device information (OS version, device model) for crash reporting and compatibility purposes.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate and improve the App, respond to support requests, send important service updates, and ensure the security of your account.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard encryption to protect data in transit and at rest. We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide you with our services.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting (e.g. Firebase, Crashlytics). These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To make a request, please contact us at the address below. You may also delete your account directly within the App.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@sulacodigital.com.
